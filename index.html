📈 Finding 1 — Athlete Diversity Is Rising

Simpson Diversity Index has climbed steadily across all three sports since 2012, with Women’s Basketball leading (+7pp). Roster demographic composition is becoming more representative of the general population.

SDI Rising Across All Sports

🚀 Finding 2 — Expansion Without Elevation

WBB’s assistant-to-head coach ratio grew from 3.04 (2012) to 3.92 (2025) — more assistants are being hired, but head coach slots are not expanding proportionally. Staff growth is not translating to leadership access.

Ratio +0.88 pts in 13 Years

⚖ Finding 3 — Coaching Has Not Diversified at the Same Pace

White coaches remain overrepresented relative to athletes. Across all three sports, the White coach-to-athlete representation gap is positive and persistent, indicating systemic barriers in the coaching pipeline.

White Coach Overrepresentation Persists

♀ Finding 4 — Female Head Coaching Leadership

Women remain the majority of coaches across the three women’s sports, but representation in WBB has plateaued around ~65%, suggesting a structural ceiling in leadership progression.

~65% Women Coaches (WBB)

🚫 Finding 5 — Promotion Bottlenecks: The Signature Finding

Coaches of color — particularly Black/AA women — are well-represented at the assistant level but sharply underrepresented at the head coach level. This head-vs-assistant promotion gap is the clearest evidence of a structural barrier in the pipeline. A gap ≥10 percentage points flags systemic disparity, not random variation.

➜ Black/AA: Higher Asst % than Head Coach %

📚 Metrics & Definitions

Reference guide to key metrics used throughout this dashboard.

MetricDefinitionUnit
Simpson Diversity Index (SDI) Probability that two randomly selected individuals from a group belong to different demographic (race/ethnicity) categories. Computed per sport, role, and year from EADA headcount data. 0 – 1 (higher = more diverse)
Coach–Athlete Gap Coach race % minus Athlete race % for the same sport and year. Positive values indicate coaches of that race are overrepresented relative to the athlete population. Percentage points (pp)
Promotion Gap Head Coach race % minus Assistant Coach race %. Negative values indicate a group is underrepresented at the Head Coach level relative to their presence in the assistant coach pipeline. Percentage points (pp)
Assistant-to-Head Ratio Number of assistant coach positions per head coach position in a given sport and year. A rising ratio indicates staff growth without a proportional expansion in head coach slots. Ratio (x)

⚠ A gap of ±10 percentage points or greater is flagged as a structural disparity throughout this dashboard — indicating a difference unlikely to be explained by random variation alone.

Data source: NCAA Demographics Database, 2011–12 to 2024–25, published September 2025 on NCAA.org
